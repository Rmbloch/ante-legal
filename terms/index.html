Ante is a personal screen-time tool. You choose which of your own apps to monitor and set a daily time limit for them. While you have minutes available for an app, you can wager them in in-app mini-games using an in-app, on-device unit we call "minutes": winning a wager adds to your available minutes for that day, and losing one subtracts the amount you staked.
